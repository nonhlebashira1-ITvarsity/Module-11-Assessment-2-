Boucheri Union Square New York


The bun coating is like nothing I’ve seen or eaten before,so glossy and sharp looking it’s almost like glass!

It’s not heavy at all though, light and crispy. The seasoning that the hot fried beef patty is doused in is

also like a first for me, super spicy,then sweet, then spicy again. The cool ranch is actually perfect, nothing

more to say here. The slaw and pickles are epic too.The pickles are thick cut so they’re much more of

a feature in the burger. Also got to have the tenders, mozza sticks and wings — all phenomenal.

Seriously, this is something special, just go get it. Visit the place and thank me later.
